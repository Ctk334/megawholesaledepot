Wholesale Depot

Welcome to Mega Wholesales Depot, one the leading wholesale and hardware supplier in Zimbabwe. We are a family-owned and operated business that was established in 2010 by Machael Mawere, who had a vision to provide quality products and services to the wholesale and hardware industry in Zimbabwe. We source out goods that are sold in supermarkets by getting them from manufacturers, and put them at our wholesale warehouses where people come to buy supplying their supermarkets and even ordering. People also come to buy in our wholesale all over the nation. We offer a wide range of products, including groceries, household items, hardware tools, electrical equipment, plumbing supplies, and more. We also provide delivery services, installation services, and customer support to ensure your satisfaction is guranteed. Whether you are a supermarket owner, a hardware store owner, or a general consumer, you can trust us to provide you with the best wholesale and hardware solutions in Zimbabwe. We invite you to browse our website, visit our warehouses, or contact us to learn more about our products and services. We look forward to serving you and exceeding your expectations. Our mission is to help our customers grow their businesses and save money by offering them the best products and prices in the market. We are committed to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We value our relationships with our customers, suppliers, and employees, and we strive to create a positive and friendly environment for everyone.
